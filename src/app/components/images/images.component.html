Home made soaps

Our soaps are made from the basis of olive oil, in the traditional old-fashioned way. They are completely handmade and every product is unique souvenir with a slightly different shape and color.

Four different scents

The flavours of our soaps are inspired from the Mediterranean environment in which we live in. Rosemary, lavander, lemon and orange are among the most characteristic plants of this area, with it's distinct scent and flavour.

Souvenir package

Soaps are wrapped in straw, with a slightly different pattern of every product, among with the informative label. It makes our product a nicely shaped object, which can work both as a piece of cosmetics, as an original souvenir from Brac island.
